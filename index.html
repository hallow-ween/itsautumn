It is called autumn

it is not "fall", it is autumn.

Autumn

noun

the season between summer and winter
comprising in the northern hemisphere
usually the months of September,
October, and November or as reckoned
astronomically extending from the September equinox to the December solstice

The word autumn derives from the Latin word autumnus, which means 'the passing of the year'.
the word appeared in the late 1300s as an adaptation of the old french word autumpne.
The more common (and inferior) term fall was coined nearly three centuries later.
The word autumn was interconnected with harvest, so other than being the original word, it also is associated with the cozy season we love!
The Autumnal Equinox falls on September 22nd (Northen Hemesphere) and marks the start of autumn. You can use the button down below to check if it is autumn or not.
