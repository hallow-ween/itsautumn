It's Called Autumn!!!

It is called autumn

Not "fall", it is autumn.

The word autumn derives from the Latin word autumnus, which means 'the passing of the year'.
the word appeared in the late 1300s as an adaptation of the old french word autumpne.
The more common (and inferior) term fall was coined nearly three centuries later.
The word autumn was interconnected with harvest, so other than being the "OG" word, it also is associated with the cozy season we love!
